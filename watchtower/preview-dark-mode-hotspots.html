What "comb through" would mean

A grep through index.html for inline styles containing hex literals like #e0f2fe, #fef2f2, #dcfce7, #fef3c7, etc. — finding every spot like the LEFT column above and rewriting it to look like the RIGHT column. Probably 30-50 small edits, mostly in the per-probe drawer renderers (renderWsb, renderOmsa, renderBackup, etc.) and the Settings tab callouts. Nothing's broken either way — the LEFT column is still readable in dark mode — it just looks off because the callout backgrounds stay light against the dark surrounding surface.
