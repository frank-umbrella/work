Watchtower logo pulse previews

Five animation variants on the same crenellated-tower mark used in the header (24 px), drawer, login card, and favicon. Each card shows the variant at three sizes plus a header mock so you can compare them at the actual UX scale. Pick one — or mix.

All variants respect prefers-reduced-motion.
